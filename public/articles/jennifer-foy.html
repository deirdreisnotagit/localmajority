Background: Democratic Candidate Jennifer Carroll Foy

Jennifer is a public defender, and she represents adolescents, the indigent, and the mentally ill. She was one of the first female graduates from Virginia Military Institute. She created the Foundation for Foster and Orphan Children and is a foster parent herself.
