Lee Carter

Lee is an IT specialist and now repairs oncology equipment. He is a worker’s candidate. He said: “My campaign is staying focused on kitchen table issues. Workers in our state worry about how they’re going to stay healthy, put food on the table, keep a roof over their heads, send their kids to school, and retire with dignity.

He is an advocate of campaign finance reform and limiting the influence of corporations in Virginia politics. He is one of more than 60 Democratic candidates that has pledged to reject donations from utilities like Dominion Energy and Appalachian Power. He is running a people-funded campaign that is not beholden to business interests, unlike the Republican incumbent.

Lee’s focus will be on finding solutions to traffic congestion such as expanding mass transit and the services of the Virginia Railway Express. He supports a woman’s right to choose, equal pay for equal work, increased protection from domestic violence. He believes in rehabilitation over incarceration for non-violent crimes because that is less harmful to minority communities.
